gemtc.drugis.org

Bayesian network meta-analysis

gemtc.drugis.org enables you to perform Bayesian network meta-analysis. You can upload your own dataset and perform the various analyses supported by the GeMTC R package. GeMTC is also a key component of the ADDIS 2 decision support system for health care policy. If you already have a dataset ready for analysis, and only want to perform a network meta-analysis, you can use this stand-alone version instead of the full ADDIS 2 software. gemtc.drugis.org is currently in early development.

You need a Google account to sign in. The information we retrieve from Google is only used to ensure that you continue to have access to the projects you create.
